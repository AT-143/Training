About Us

Learn about our journey, mission, and values

Our Story

Founded in 2024, AT Cloud Kitchen started with a simple yet ambitious dream: to bring authentic South Indian cuisine to the heart of Rajamahendravarm. Our founder, Venkata Anjesh Tummala, wanted to create a space where people could experience the rich, bold flavors of traditional South Indian dishes made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

What began as a small family-owned restaurant has now grown into a beloved establishment, serving thousands of happy customers every year. From the first crispy dosa to our signature biryanis, we continue to craft every dish with love, passion, and authenticity.

Our Mission

At AT Cloud Kitchen, our mission is to offer the most authentic South Indian food experience outside of India. We aim to deliver fresh, flavorful, and high-quality dishes in a welcoming atmosphere where guests can enjoy a true taste of South India.

We are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality, service, and hospitality to make every visit a memorable one.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We take pride in serving authentic, flavorful dishes made with traditional recipes passed down through generations.
  • Quality: We source the finest ingredients to ensure that every dish is of the highest quality and tastes as it should.
  • Hospitality: We believe that great food deserves great service. Our staff is committed to making every guest feel welcomed and valued.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental footprint by using eco-friendly packaging and supporting sustainable farming practices.
